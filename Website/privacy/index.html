Privacy Policy

Effective January 1st, 2019

We collect certain information or data about you when you use portal.studentpath.co.uk, www.studentpath.co.uk, studentpath.co.uk or any studentpath.co.uk sub-domains.

When registering on our site, as appropriate, you may be asked to enter names, dates or other details to help you with your experience.

This data can be viewed by authorised people at Blooware Technologies Limited, to:

● Improve the site by monitoring how you use it.
● Gather feedback to improve our services.
● Respond to any feedback you send us, if you’ve asked us to.
● Send email alerts to users who request them.

Where Your data is stored:

We store your data on secure, encrypted (AES-256) servers in the Google Cloud, more specifically in the London, England data centre (europe-west2-b). By submitting your personal data, you agree to this.

Email alerts:

By submitting your data you agree to be automatically signed up to email alerts. We do not contact any students, only StudentPath account holders (School administrators etc).

Data Security:

Sending information over the internet is generally not completely secure, and we can’t guarantee the security of your data while it’s in transit. Any data you send is at your own risk. We have procedures and security features in place to keep your data secure once we receive it. These procedures are in line with modern industry standards and continue to be refined and improved upon internally. All code and data is stored within the UK. No aspect of the system leaves the United Kingdom’s WAN (Wider area Network).

Disclosing Your information:

We may pass on your personal information if we have a legal obligation to do so, or if we have to enforce or apply our terms of use and other agreements. We won’t share your information with any other organisations for marketing, market research or commercial purposes, and we don’t pass on your details to third party websites.

Your Rights:

You can find out what information we hold about you, and ask us not to use any of the information we collect. You can unsubscribe to emails or remove your data from our servers by submitting a request email to contact@blooware.co.uk

Links to other websites

If you go to another website from this one, read the privacy policy on that website to find out what it does with your information.
